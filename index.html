About Us

Local, Reliable,
Right on the N2

N2 Secure Storage is based in Albertinia, a small town in the Western Cape situated halfway between Mossel Bay and Riversdale on the N2 national highway. That central location makes us easy to reach whether you're a local resident, a passing traveller, or a business operating along the Garden Route corridor.

We offer honest, no-fuss storage with flexible monthly agreements. Whether you're between moves, need to store business stock, or want a safe home for your trailer or caravan, we've got you covered.
